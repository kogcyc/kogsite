How To Order Goods

We do business in an old-fashioned way: we take orders via telephone calls and we ship orders via the US Postal Service. So if you see something that interests you on our list of good then please call us between the hours of 6:00 and 17:00 Pacific Time at (+1) 341-333-8900. If you want to have a brief conversation about ordering or orders or if you have a quick question then please text us at that same number.

About Us

Kogswell Cycles is a small business that focuses on helping commuters and other working-class cyclists obtain, maintain and use high quality bicycles. We strive to listen to what customers need and we do that by conversation.
